CHPIA 2023

In November 2023, I had the privilege of attending the 3rd International Conference on Public Health in Africa (CPHIA 2023) in Lusaka, Zambia. The conference, themed "Breaking Barriers: Repositioning Africa in the Global Health Architecture," brought together over 5,000 participants, including policymakers, researchers, and health professionals from across the continent and beyond. Engaging in sessions on digital innovation and health systems strengthening, I gained valuable insights into Africa's evolving public health landscape and the collaborative efforts driving transformative change. This experience has further fueled my commitment to leveraging data analytics and innovative solutions to address public health challenges in our communities.
